DuPont Corian offers more choices than any other surface material you can buy.  A wide variety of colors.  Edge treatments.  Decorative inlays.  Corian sinks can be combined with Corian countertops to create seamless-looking, color-coordinated worktops.  Corian can even be formed and shaped into sweeping curves and angles.

Better yet is the inner beauty of Corian - its ability to stand up to life's daily calamities.  Corian is nonporous, which makes it easy to clean.  It resists stains because liquids can't penetrate.  When Corian looks clean, it really is clean.

Corian keeps its good looks longer because the solid beauty of Corian goes all the way through.
