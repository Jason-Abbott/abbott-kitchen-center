Our 10-step process includes hand sanding, hand-wiped stain, two coats of sealer (each which is oven baked, then hand sanded) and a Catalyzed Coversion Top Coat with UV protectant (also oven baked).

Our sealer and top coat contain more solids, providing better protection against staining and fading then most other cabinet finishes.
